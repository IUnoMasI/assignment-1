The Adeptus Astartes

The Adeptus Astartes are the choosen warriors of the God Emperor of Man in the Warhammer 40k Universe

The Adeptus Astartes, or more commonly known as Space Marines, are an elite faction of soldiers who serve the God Emperor of Man Kind. They are strong, they are smart, they are fast, and most importantly, THEY HAVE FAITH IN THE EMPEROR! FOR THE EMPEROR!

These soldiers are picked from varying planets around the galaxy and are the best of the best, the cream of the crop. Some of their hobbies include purging heretics, slaying ALL xenos, fighting the daemons of chaos, and praying to the Emperor.
